Lost!

TOGGLE SOUND

As the party stepped into the entrance of the ancient labyrinth, they were filled with excitement and anticipation. The quest they embarked upon was to find the legendary "Eye of the Oracle," a crystal said to hold the wisdom of ages. Its knowledge was the key to solving a growing crisis in their kingdom. At first, the maze seemed like a straightforward challenge. The party marked their path with chalk and tied strings to branches, ensuring they could find their way back. However, as they delved deeper into the labyrinth's twisting passages, the walls seemed to shift and change, confounding their attempts at navigation. "What's going on? This path was clear just moments ago!" one party member exclaimed, glancing back in confusion. Another member pointed at the markings they had left on the wall. "Look! The chalk lines have disappeared, and our strings have vanished!"

The labyrinth was playing tricks on them, erasing their attempts to find a way out. Panic began to set in as they retraced their steps, only to encounter the same junctions over and over again. "We have to stay calm," the party's leader said, trying to steady her voice. "We can't afford to lose hope. Let's gather our thoughts and think this through." But the maze was unforgiving, leading them further astray. They tried leaving breadcrumbs, but the maze seemed to consume them. It became clear that the labyrinth was enchanted, a sentient puzzle determined to keep intruders trapped. Hours turned into days as the party wandered in a seemingly endless loop of dead ends and retraced steps. Exhaustion and frustration grew, and the morale began to wane. They felt like pawns in some malevolent game, toys for the labyrinth's amusement.
